The Initiative

Where Innovation
Meets Global Opportunity

FoundryBridge is a venture creation initiative focused on bridging Israeli deep-tech health innovation into global markets. It operates at the intersection of technology leadership, venture ecosystem building, and business strategy — engaging startups, venture firms, and innovation teams to create meaningful impact at scale.

Deep-Tech Health

Focusing on breakthrough Israeli innovation at the intersection of life sciences, medical devices, digital health, and advanced biotech.

Global Market Bridge

Translating Israeli early-stage startups into internationally scalable ventures — connecting technology to capital and commercial opportunity.

Ecosystem Architecture

Building the connective tissue between startups, venture firms, strategic corporates, and innovation programs across markets and geographies.

About

Yinon Amir

Yinon Amir is a technology leader and venture ecosystem builder operating at the intersection of deep-tech innovation, venture capital, and business strategy. Established Philips for Startups Israel - the local corporate innovation and strategy arm - technology scouting, ecosystem partnerships, and deal flow execution.

With 25+ years spanning R&D leadership at Philips, DarioHealth, and Amdocs — and as co-founder and CEO of an early-stage digital health startup — he brings both operator depth and founder perspective to every engagement. Known for building and scaling high-performing engineering organizations, driving corporate innovation, and connecting deep-tech with commercial outcomes.

Currently active as Venture Partner at NIT — Northern Israeli Tech Fund, supporting health-tech and deep-tech deal flow and investment evaluation.
