The Initiative

From Science to Venture

FoundryBridge exists to close the gap between breakthrough science and successful healthcare ventures at scale — working at every stage from discovery to formation to ecosystem connection

Discover

Identifying promising science is only the beginning. The real challenge is recognizing where innovation has the potential to become a successful healthcare venture.

Form

Scientific breakthroughs become successful ventures by systematically closing the commercial gap — through commercial design and strategy from day one.

Connect

Successful ventures grow through the right relationships — connecting founders with the expertise, partners, and ecosystem that help move innovation forward.

HOW WE WORK

Our Practice

Network and Direct Access

Direct working relationships with 18 university and hospital TTO pipelines across Israel's leading academic and clinical institutions — deal flow at the formation stage, before it reaches the broader market.

Formation and Build

We build alongside founders — co-forming ventures with scientist-entrepreneurs and clinician-founders, providing the commercial design, market strategy, and validation infrastructure that bridges deep-tech IP from raw academic asset to investable company.

Healthcare Venture Intelligence

Working alongside venture funds and founders on technology scouting, opportunity assessment, and due diligence — surfacing and evaluating emerging health-tech innovation before it reaches institutional visibility.

Technology Depth

Deep technology leadership across early-stage and enterprise health-tech — grounding every venture we help create in technical credibility and operational depth.

FoundryBridge engages at the formation stage — partnering with founders, academic tech transfer offices, and clinician-innovators before a company has fully formed, establishing the commercial, technical, and strategic foundations that institutional investors expect.

Capital Efficiency and Structural Upside — Academic IP arrives with years of R&D already absorbed — typically representing $5M+ of prior development investment. This structural maturity reduces capital burn, enables higher ownership in a de-risked opportunity, and shortens time to institutional funding milestones.
